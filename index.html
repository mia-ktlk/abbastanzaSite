About Us

Abbastanza was founded by Jennifer Kotalik with the mission of changing the conversation around body image and acceptance. Starting small, her team is growing into a dedicated community who work together to encourage wellness from within. We work to combat eating disorders, body dysmorphia, and other mental health problems caused by the myth of body perfection. Through education, coaching, and community outreach, we at Abbastanza strive to help members of our community know that they are enough.

About Jennifer



Jennifer is a RN of 20 years, ACE Health Coach, and Personal Trainer. Jennifer founded Abbastanza after 34 years of experience in the health & fitness industry. Jennifer taught group fitness in Westchester, NY from 1985-1997, before moving to Flagstaff, AZ where she has taught continually since then. With Abbastanza she is dedicated to changing the conversation around body image and acceptance.

Strength and Fexibility

Do you wish to build strength and flexibility?

Abbastanza strength and flexibility training sessions are available for small groups, couples or individuals. Training with Jennifer Kotalik is based in training smarter, setting attainable goals, and working with a Body Positive Philosophy.
